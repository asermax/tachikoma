### Flagged Unknowns

#### S8 — Post-processing resumption awareness
The mechanism for communicating resumption boundaries to post-processing forks is unclear. The `fork_and_consume()` pattern gives the forked agent the full transcript — we need a way to indicate "only extract content after [timestamp]." Options include adding resumption metadata to the `Session` dataclass, querying `SessionResumption` records from within the processor, or augmenting the fork prompt. Needs investigation during design.
